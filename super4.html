Aquaman

Character Profile

Real Name: Arthur Curry

Height: 6'3"

Weight: 350 lbs

Eye Colour: Blue

Hair Colour: Blonde

Identity: Public

Place Of Birth: Amenesty Bay, Maine U.S.A

Citizenship: U.S Citizen, Atlantean

Base: Formerly Poseidonis, Amnesty Bay

Education: High School; Tutored by Atlantean Scholars

Occupation: Atlantean King, Superhero



Powers

Aquaman has the the ability to access "The Clear" which Swamp Thing and Animal Man also came in contact with as well. With Arthur being able to access the Clear he has the ability to sense primal emotions of aquatic creatures.
